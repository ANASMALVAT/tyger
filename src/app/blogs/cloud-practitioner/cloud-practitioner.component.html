What is AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification?

With the rise of Cloud Computing, companies have constantly been shifting from running their infrastructure on-premise, to running them on cloud, which offers far more elasticity in terms of scaling and resiliency in case a disaster strikes.

The AWS Cloud Practitioner certification validates your foundational knowledge of AWS cloud services and provides a solid understanding of how to navigate the AWS platform. This certification is designed for individuals who are new to the cloud and want to gain a broad understanding of AWS services, architectural best practices, and cloud concepts. It equips you with the skills to identify AWS services, understand basic architectural principles, and comprehend the value proposition of AWS. Prepare to explore the world of cloud computing and take your first step towards becoming a proficient AWS Cloud Practitioner.

Exam Format

The AWS Cloud Practitioner exam comprises 65 questions that need to be answered within a time limit of 90 minutes. This allows you an average of around 1 minute and 23 seconds per question. To pass the exam, you will need to achieve a minimum score of 700 out of a possible scale of 100 to 1000. For a successful outcome, you should aim to answer a significant portion of the questions correctly.

Taking the Test

You have two options for taking the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification exam: offline at a testing center or online from the convenience of your home. If there are testing centers available in your vicinity, I recommend selecting the offline option. However, if there are no nearby testing centers or you prefer the flexibility of an online exam, you can choose the online option.

Keep in mind that the online test has strict proctoring measures in place. If the proctor cancels your exam due to any violations, a refund will not be provided. In such cases, you will need to reschedule and retake the exam at a later date.
