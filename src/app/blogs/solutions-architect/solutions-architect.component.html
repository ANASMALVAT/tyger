What is AWS Solutions Architect Associate Certification?

With the rise of Cloud Computing, companies have constantly been shifting from running their infrastructure on-premise, to running them on cloud, which offers far more elasticity in terms of scaling and resiliency in case a disaster strikes.

The AWS Solutions Architect - Associate certification validates your ability to design and deploy well-architected solutions on AWS, which is the leading cloud provider today. In simple terms, this exam tests your ability to propose an architecture given a specific scenario. For example: a company wants their application to continue running even if an entire AWS region, where that application was hosted, is down due to a disaster. So, how would you design their infrastructure around this use case?

For a comprehensive understanding of the AWS Solutions Architect - Associate concepts, I enrolled in Stephane Maarek's highly acclaimed course available on Udemy. Taking the Test

You have two options for taking the AWS Solutions Architect - Associate certification exam: offline at a testing center or online from the comfort of your home. If testing centers are available in your area, I recommend choosing the offline option. However, if there are no nearby testing centers, you can opt for the online exam.

Keep in mind that the online test has strict proctoring measures in place. If the proctor cancels your exam due to any violations, a refund will not be provided. In such cases, you will need to reschedule and retake the exam at a later date.

Tips for taking the AWS SAA exam

To excel in the AWS Solutions Architect - Associate certification exam, I recommend taking multiple practice tests. These tests will familiarize you with common exam topics and closely mirror the actual exam questions.
